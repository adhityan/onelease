JOB OFFER | betahaus Accelerator Assistant 

Are you looking for a job? Do you have a bachelor’s degree in business or a related field and do you know all the ins and outs of the network startup ecosystems? Do you have an entrepreneurial mindset and minimum 1 year working experience in Management Consulting or Finance? Read on, read on, because we might be looking for you!

At betahaus we strive to provide the best place for startups. A part of this is to create a great network and provide the opportunity for startups to join accelerators that happen in our space or are built by us. We’re looking for an accelerator assistant to be the right hand of our accelerator manager in a 3-month, top of the line accelerator program.

The essence of the accelerator program is to identify the most important needs of the startups participating, and to then build a program and a network to meet their needs. As our accelerator assistant, you’ll be responsible for both organization and content. You’ll have a lot to do, but you’ll also have a lot of influence on the program: to shape it and make it the best it can possibly be!

YOU’LL BE IN CHARGE OF | 

Programming, Representation, Network & Community

  • Identify and monitor startup ecosystem for up and coming players to involve in the accelerator; relevant events, network players, HR, etc.
  • Assisting startups with their questions and needs; operational things, commodities and accelerator related activities
  • Coordinating media request, taking care of social media
  • Collecting feedback from participants & poll availability of mentors and coaches

Event management

  • Setting up space and equipment, booking resources & equipment
  • Coordinating accelerator activities (meetings with stakeholders, catering for events)
  • Backing up the general manager (e.g. on relevant events & meetups)
  • Taking part in all program activities to ensure quality

Operations

  • Documenting the accelerator program (photo’s, videos, etc.)
  • Creation of accounting reports and other documents needed
  • Writing reports and coordinating with project management
  • Translate from English to German if needed
  • In case of emergency; be ready for action

WE’RE LOOKING FOR YOU! | 

We are looking for junior candidates with a talent for operations and groundwork and at least an entrepreneurial background.

  • Bachelor degree in business or related field
  • Minimum 1 year of work experience in management consulting, finance
  • Knowledge of network startup ecosystem
  • Entrepreneurial mindset
  • Problem solving skills and hands-on approach
  • Ability to work collaboratively and build trust

Do you have the skills and are you excited to get on board? Send us your CV and letter of intent (in one pdf. file please!) at contact@betahaus.de

Starting date: As soon as possible
Duration: 3 months

The deadline for sending in your application is June 15. We will then screen the applications and get back to you as soon as possible! Looking forward to hearing with you,

Yours truly,

The betahaus team
