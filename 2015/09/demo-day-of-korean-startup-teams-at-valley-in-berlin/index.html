Demo Day of Korean startup teams at Valley in Berlin

Ladies and Gents, on September 24th, the international investor scene and Berlin startups will come together at the renowned “Valley in Berlin” and we want to see you there!

In the last weeks you may have seen numerous mentors flowing into the house and talking to the teams of Korean startups, who are staying at betahaus for their 3-month acceleration program. This buzz is coming to its climax. On September 24th, a Demo Day takes place during the international Valley in Berlin conference! Investors, VCs & angels from Silicon Valley and South Korea will spend the whole day networking and building bridges.

Hosting the event are the local startup hubs: betahaus with Accelerate Korea, Founder Institute and YOU IS NOW which hosts the event.

The joint conference will welcome a wealth of selected guests, speakers and investors, flying in specially from Silicon Valley and South Korea. All day Valley in Berlin will be hosting talks, pitches and sessions on accelerators, startup ecosystem in Asia and community engagement. Startups from various accelerator programs & countries will pitch what they’ve been working on. But that’s not all. Parties with startups are always legendary and often lead to foundations with big partnerships, so stay for a drink afterwards!

Pitching will be performed by our 5 Korean startups alongside 6 European teams from You Is Now & Founders Institute. International teams will show you what’s their piece of innovation and you’ll have the chance to compare the trends.

These five Korean rockstar teams have been with us at betahaus this summer: BEE CANVAS, Equipment Master, BBuzzArt, RX Cycle and Lollicam. They spent the last months identifying the market differences, refining products and tweaking their pitches. Now it’s time to show everyone what they’ve got. From the future of indoor cycling, to the new way to organize your info online, and the peer2peer platform for heavy construction machinery, the teams will present the latest from tech and innovation in Asia.

With over 500 guests set to attend, this day will be a melting pot of bright minds from Europe, Asia and the States. Head over to the website for the full program.

We have free tickets for the community, grab one before they are all snapped up!

See you Thursday September 24th @ the Valley in Berlin!
