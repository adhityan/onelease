We’re opening our café till 10pm!

Just as the summer approaches we have some news to bright up your days & nights.

Opening hours of our café were prolonged and now you can stay & drink in our café every work day till 10 pm!

Monday – Friday: 8 am – 10 pm

Saturday: 10 am – 8 pm

Beer & wine is served all day long which gives you the chance to finally drink & work as Hemingway or simply stay for after-work beers.

Our front yard is also getting ready for the summer and gets more tables for bigger lunches on the sun.

We’re ready for the summer, are you too?

See you at us!
