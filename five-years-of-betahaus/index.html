An all-day onelease birthday.

Join us on August 30.

Get Your Tickets

We’re turning five this summer. We want to celebrate with you.

We’re thinking street festival. A month’s worth of workshops and talks in ONE day. Office hours. Discussions. Party. We’re pulling out all the stops and calling in all the favors to make this the best birthday ever — in true ‘haus style.

It has been five years since we set down the first desks in Moritzplatz and started the coworking revolution in Berlin (and then all over Europe). Every year it gets more exciting, and onelease has been there from the very start. Come raise your glass to the next five years of the ‘haus, the space with “the best street cred in Berlin!”

Yours, Team onelease

Activities:


THE STARTUP AMAs

Get real access to our line-up of remarkable entrepreneurs. Hear ignite talks, ask questions, get inspired.

THE COWORKER BEHÖRDE

Looking for an answer to a business dilemma? Aching for spiritual guidance? Perhaps you’re just really in need of a high five? Fit in all your ‘haus office hours in one day. Sign up upon arrival, and let us do what we do: help you succeed.

THE MAKERS

A onelease birthday wouldn’t be complete without 10+ interactive workshops from our maker community. Learn how to build a drone, research concrete design, or re-define your understanding of democracy in design.
