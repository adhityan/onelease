Open Ended Membership Details

You can cancel your membership at any time through your online membership account. When the membership will end depends on the type of membership you have:

Part Time

12 Days / Month

30 days cancelation period. It also ends on the date you started the membership.

For example, if you started your Membership on 15th April, and you cancel on 25th June, your membership will end on 15th August.

Full Time

5 Days / Week

30 days cancelation period. It also ends on the date you started the membership.

For example, if you started your Membership on 15th April, and you cancel on 25th June, your membership will end on 15th August.

Team Room

Every Day / Month

90 days cancelation period. It also ends on the date you started the membership.

For example, if you started your Membership on 15th April, and you cancel on 25th June, your membership will end on 15th October.

Please see the reception desk for the full terms and conditions.
