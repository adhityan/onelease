Residencies at the Hardware.co Lab

Our woodshop provides a professional workspace for carpenters, product designers, and other wood experts. The woodshop has space for up to 10 members and is equipped with high-quality power tools and hand tools from Festool. The woodshop is managed by a team of highly skilled woodworkers and is open to members on weekdays from 9am-5pm. In the evenings and on weekends the woodshop hosts various workshops and courses created by betahaus I Academy.

 

Pricing and options

Day pass – 29 euro

Monthly membership – 309 euro

3-Monthly membership – 850 euro

 

Do you want to buy a day pass? You can just walk in and buy your pass for the day at our reception.
Are you interested in becoming a member? Great! We’d love to have you on board. Feel free to drop by any time during opening hours, or click the APPLY & BOOK button, fill in the form and we’ll get back to you with three work days.

 

Be aware that you can only buy a day pass or become a member when you have experience working with power tools, or when you have attended our Introduction to Woodshop taking place every other weekend.
