Working with Wood

Event Description

Hands on wood experience

Got a cool woodworking project that you want to start? Perhaps you want to join some of our woodworking evenings? Well, you’ll need access to professional tools, and know how to use them!

In this two-day course for rookies, Lluis will give you a basic introduction into the machines and tools in the betahaus woodshop.

But it won’t just be learning the basics and getting to know the machines, you will be building your own cool piece of furniture to be used as a Laptop desk or just a beautiful wooden bed tray. Through a shared, basic, and take-home project you will acquire enough skills to start working on simple projects afterwards.

SESSION OVERVIEW

First Session, Friday: You will get to know the material and dig in into planning. How to approach the project with sketch. Once you perfected your design on paper you will transfer the measurements on the wood piece. How to measure the right way, calculate material thickness and offcuts. In the last stage of first session you will make your first cut and learn who to securely use the table saw.

Second Session, Saturday: On the second sessions you will continue working on your cuts and trying out different machines like miter saw, the drill press and sanders. The time to assemble your table design will come. Learn to make different joints the advantages and disadvantages of different joint approaches. To finish your project you will sand it, polish it and correct any mistakes. Voila your table is ready to be used.

INSTRUCTOR: Lluis Mateu

Lluis Mateu is a woodworking enthusiast with more than 25 years of experience. He has built everything from wooden houses and furniture to sets for theatre and cinema productions! He has a deep passion for woodwork and loves sharing his excitement for the craft – as well as all the tricks he’s learned throughout the years!

FREE VOUCHERS FOR WEEKLY WOODWORKING:

Purchase of Ticket includes a 3-times Weekly Woodworking free Voucher, valid for three Months after completing the course. To collect your earned Voucher, which you will get from Lluis after you’ve finished the course.

MATERIALS:

  • one sheet of 1200 x 300 mm Leimholz buche (18mm thick)
  • Ponal glue

You can either buy them yourself or you can order them with us for 18€.


QUESTIONS?

Drop us a line at academy@betahaus.de

Please print your ticket and bring it to the workshop!
