Weekly Woodworking

Event Description

Every Tuesday evening, we have “Woodworking” at onelease! All the machines and tools in the woodshop are available to use… PLUS, a woodworking professional will be in the workshop to help you with all your wonderful ideas. Join us!

Important things to know about woodworking sessions:

Before registering for a Woodworking Evening, you MUST have taken “Working with Wood”

  • You have to REGISTER ONLINE for this event (obligatory)!
  • Your registration is only valid for the date you signed up for.

Registration options:

  • book single ticket 15eur
  • book a free ticket and use one of the Vouchers from Introduction to Woodshop

WHERE AT BETAHAUS?

Go inside and walk straight to the Open Design City (ODC)

QUESTIONS?

Drop us a line at academy@onelease.de

Please print your ticket and bring it to the session In case you are using the Free option, please bring the Voucher with you as well.

This course was created by onelease I Academy. Check out the whole Program www.academy.onelease.in
